Our Services

EGHI provides the following services and activities:

  • Educational events and guest lectures on gut health topics
  • Volunteer opportunities in healthcare settings
  • Gut-healthy cooking workshops and demonstrations
  • Community outreach programs
  • Educational resources and blog content
  • Merchandise and food sales at select events

Educational Nature

All content provided by EGHI is for educational purposes only. We do not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

Membership & Participation

Free Membership

EGHI membership is completely free and open to all Emory University students, regardless of major or year. By joining EGHI, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information during registration
  • Respect all members and maintain professional conduct
  • Follow Emory University's code of conduct
  • Participate in activities safely and responsibly

Photography & Media

By participating in EGHI events, you consent to being photographed or recorded. These materials may be used for:

  • EGHI website and social media
  • Promotional materials
  • Educational presentations

If you prefer not to be photographed, please inform event organizers.

User Content & Submissions

Blog Submissions

Members may submit blog posts for consideration. By submitting content, you:

  • Grant EGHI the right to review, edit, and publish your content
  • Confirm that the content is original and doesn't infringe on others' rights
  • Understand that publication is at EGHI's discretion
  • Allow EGHI to use your name and affiliation with proper attribution

Content Standards

All submitted content must be:

  • Accurate and evidence-based
  • Respectful and appropriate
  • Free from plagiarism
  • Relevant to gut health and wellness
