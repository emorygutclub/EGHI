Expert Guest Lectures

Connect with leading gastroenterologists, researchers, and healthcare professionals through our monthly hybrid lecture series. These sessions are designed to be interactive. Come ready to ask questions, engage in discussion, and satisfy your curiosity about all things gut health. Whether you join in person or virtually, you'll have the opportunity to learn directly from experts and explore topics that spark your interest.

Make a real impact in the Atlanta community through hands-on volunteer opportunities. Members participate in food bank initiatives and gut health education outreach, helping spread awareness about digestive wellness to those who need it most. It's a meaningful way to apply what you're learning while giving back, and earning service hours along the way.

These workshops are completely free for members, making delicious gut health accessible to everyone.
