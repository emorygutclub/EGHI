Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join EGHI? +
Joining EGHI is easy! Simply fill out our membership form on our website or attend one of our general body meetings. We welcome all Emory students regardless of major or year. Membership includes access to all events, workshops, and volunteering opportunities.
When are your meetings? +
Our general body meetings schedule has not been formulated yet. When we decide, we will post it to this site!
Do I need a medical background to join? +
Not at all! EGHI welcomes students from all academic backgrounds. Whether you're pre-med, studying public health, business, or any other field, you'll find valuable opportunities to learn about gut health and its impact on overall wellness. Our programming is designed to be accessible to everyone.
How can I become a speaker or partner with EGHI? +
We're always looking for expert speakers and partnership opportunities! Please use our contact form above and select "Speaker Request" or "Partnership Opportunity" as your inquiry type. Include information about your expertise, proposed topics, and how you'd like to collaborate with EGHI.
What volunteering opportunities are available? +
EGHI offers various volunteering opportunities including meal prepping healthy foods for the Atlanta Food Bank and outreaching via educational programs aimed at teaching young adults the benefits of a healthy diet and healthy gut. More opportunities will be announced soon!
