Introduction

Welcome to the Emory Gut Health Initiative (EGHI) Privacy Policy. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Our Promise: We believe in transparency and will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.
